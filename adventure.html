TYOS'STELLAR ADVENTURE

Travelling Across The Cosmos

In the vastness of space and the immensity of time, it is my pleasure to share with you a journey of a lifetime. Literally.

Life is an adventure, or so they say afterall. I guess it does sound like a lame cliche, however, it would be utterly foolish to dismiss it as mere nonsense.


Now, where were we? Ah, yes. The trip encompassing a lifetime. In particular, we will take a look at a young man's pathetic and insignificant existence.
He has lived for 19 years on the third planet of the unnamed solar system of the Milky Way Galaxy. They named it Earth, if I recall correctly.


Hmm? How do I know all of this? Well, of course I would. He's me.

On the 27th Midnight of April 2003,

The entity known as Theus was born. From the depts of his mother's womb, a weak, small and disfigured creature has sprung forth. It has no recollection of the said event, but it is deemed to be an important day by society nonetheless.


It was nothing special really. My birth didn't really make a huge impact or anything.


For instance:

  • It didn't change the world
  • Didn't solve world hunger
  • Haven't caused world peace
  • Nor did it cure cancer

In the grand scheme of things, I was simply born. Regardless of that fact, or rather because of it, I was swept up in the Great Voyage that is life.
